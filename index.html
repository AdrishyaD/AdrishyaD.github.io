Joy

01 / 09

Is she a petal of joy?

Or just a fairy from my dream?

Such innocence clad eyes,

Nowhere else to be seen.

Is she a devil in disguise?

Got my heart imprisoned.

Pearls fall from the sky

When she smiles with joy.

Is she the joy herself?

Such radiance, her elegance.

Is she an eternal sunshine?

Wish if she were to be mine

Will she just remain a dream of mine?

Should I wait a little for her sign?

The Departed

02 / 09

A part of life can suddenly fall apart,

While the distance slowly grew apart.

We wish, if only the times were more apt

We would be still near, with none to depart.

The departed leaves us with a lingering heart,

Makes us realise, life's all but not a perfect art.

Her Eyes

07 / 09

The eyes, see so many eyes

Yet they end up craving, for a pair of eyes

The eyes, conceal lots of secrets

Yet, they end up revealing to the eyes

The eyes, hide the pains within

Yet, it feels for some eyes

The eyes, yearn for so many things

Yet, it finds solace in someone's eyes

The beauty lies in the eyes

All we need, a set of eyes

Deeper than the seas, her eyes!

Her eyes, those beautiful eyes!

Filled with innocence, dreams & ready to fly

I see, those eyes, her beautiful eyes!

and oh, it felt so wonderful.

There's nothing wrong being hopeful

Cause, that'd be something beautiful

That's what makes this journey worthwhile,

Keeps us journeying mile after mile.

Hey universe, would you send me a sign?

If it turns into reality this dream of mine..

The Unchanted Voice

09 / 09

The unchanted voice in my ears,

Lingers quietly within me and tears.

A dream so far from reality,

Yet a desire full of certainty.

A heart knows, what heart wants,

But does she know, what her wants?

A feeling so pure, yet at the mercy,

Still needs the grant of an agency.

A tryst of play between heart and mind,

Conflicted, yet a helpless desire of one kind..
